Component: tos.platforms.mica.MicaBusC

configuration MicaBusC

A simplistic beginning to providing a standard interface to the mica-family 51-pin bus. Just provides the PW0-PW7 and Int0-3 digital I/O pins and returns the ADC channel number for the ADC pins.
