Component: TestAdcC

module TestAdcC

Tests the AdcC subsystem and switches on leds 0, 1 and 2. if the test is successful: LED0 denotes a successful Read operation, LED1 denotes a successful ReadNow operation, LED2 denotes a successful ReadStream operation.

Jan Hauer
